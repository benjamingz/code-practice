Choosing Your Goal

  There are many, many different goals that can be achieved in the gym, and choosing the one that is right for you is important. The following are a list of possible goals, each of which will be covered. Keep in mind that not every aspiration will be addressed here, but maybe you can get an idea of where you want to head.

  1. Muscle Building
  2. Strength Building
  3. Weight Loss
  4. Weight Gain
  5. Sport Performance Enhancement
  6. Body Recomposition
  7. Health

Important Factors in Reaching Your Goal

  There are many factors that can affect your goal, but some are crucial to consider before deciding which path to follow in the gym world. Something to understand before ever beginning is that to create your ideal goal, there is no one solution, and there is no solution with one factor. For example, to lose weight, solely working out without considering diet, hydration, or sleep will no effectively decrease your weight. The things that will affect your goal are:

  1. Diet
  2. Physical Activity
  3. Sleep
  4. Hydration
